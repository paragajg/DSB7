what is horny goat weed ?
epimedium is a genus of twenty - one related plant species .
the chinese refer to epimedium as " yin yang huo " , which has been loosely translated by some as " licentious goat plant " and explains why western supplement companies have adopted the titillating name by which it is known in the u . s : ( horny goat weed ) .
epimedium is grown as an ornamental herb in asia and the mediterranean region , and various species are used for medicinal purposes , including epimedium sagittatum , epimedium brevicornum , epimedium wushanense , epimedium koreanum , and epimedium pubescens .
