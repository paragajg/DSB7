the pasadena chamber of commerce has selected the enron clean fuels methanol plant as " industry of the year " for 2001 .
the selection was based on the plant ' s community activities ; awards / recognitions received related to safety , environment and quality ; organizations supported by plant employees , and the positive impact the plant has on the local business community .
hats off to the employees of the enron clean fuels methanol plant for this special recognition !
