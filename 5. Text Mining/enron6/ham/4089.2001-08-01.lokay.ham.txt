Subject: ets day of caring
ets ' day of caring is thursday , august 9 . to date , we have over 65 ets volunteers - thank you ! more volunteers would ensure ets ' success .
on day of caring , ets will be working at the harbach ripley center located at 6225 northdale . transportation will be provided to and from the enron building to the center . bright yellow t - shirts are available for all of our ets volunteers . t - shirts can be picked up at ebl 638 e .
box lunches will be available for day of caring volunteers at the enron building . morning volunteers will get their lunch when they return to the enron building , and afternoon volunteers will get their lunch when they leave for the harbach ripley center .
specifically , during the day of caring morning and afternoon shifts , we ' ll be painting , replacing baseboards , cleaning and interacting with the seniors and the children at the center . all of these activities are indoors .
there have been several questions about whether you have to take vacation to participate in the ets august 9 day of caring . you do not have to take vacation . however , please make sure you coordinate your time out of the office during day of caring with your team and / or supervisor .
please sign up to volunteer for day of caring via the on - line link below , and indicate which shift , morning or afternoon , you prefer .
please contact kimberly nelson ( x 33580 ) with questions .
